About

Academic journey & research focus

I am an academician in Computer Science & Engineering with a strong foundation in Wireless Sensor Networks, Internet of Things, and Machine Learning. My work spans sensor deployment strategies, autonomous systems, and intelligent data-driven solutions in healthcare, agriculture, and smart environments.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering from Govind Ballabh Pant Institute of Engineering and Technology, Pauri, Garhwal (2013–2017), following an M.Tech in CSE from Maharishi Markandeshwar Engineering College (MMU), B.Tech in Information Technology from Himachal Pradesh University, and a Diploma in Information Technology from Govt. Polytechnic Hamirpur.

Core Research Interests
  • Deployment schemes and coverage strategies in Wireless Sensor Networks.
  • Machine Learning models for medical imaging, disease diagnosis and prediction.
  • IoT-based systems for water, air quality, and agricultural monitoring.
  • Automation, robotics, and glider-assisted sensor deployment.
  • Network traffic analysis, security and intelligent decision systems.
